Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and processes.

How long does a custom project take?

Custom projects typically take 2-4 weeks from initial consultation to final delivery, depending on complexity and current workload.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Contact us for specific rates.

Can I visit the studio?

Absolutely! We welcome visitors during business hours. We recommend calling ahead to ensure someone is available to show you around.

What's your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all custom pieces. Damaged items during shipping are covered by our insurance.
